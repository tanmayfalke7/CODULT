AI-POWERED CAREER PATH RECOMMENDER: DESIGN A PLATFORM THAT 
MATCHES SKILLS, INTERESTS, AND MARKET TRENDS TO CAREER OPTIONS. USE 
HYBRID ML MODELS COMBINING COLLABORATIVE FILTERING WITH LLMS FOR 
RESUME PARSING. HOST ON AWS/AZURE FOR SCALABILITY AND INTEGRATE 
INTERACTIVE VISUALIZATIONS FOR GUIDANCE. 
Overview: The proposed AI-Powered Career Path Recommender is a hybrid machine learning 
platform that integrates collaborative filtering models with Large Language Models (LLMs) 
for resume parsing and skill extraction. By combining user-specific data (skills, interests, 
academic background) with real-time labor market insights, the system will generate tailored 
career recommendations. The platform will be hosted on AWS/Azure for scalability, reliability, 
and security, and will feature interactive visualizations to help users explore career trajectories, 
required skill upgrades, and industry demand trends. 
Key Objectives / Expected Outcomes 
• Personalized Career Guidance: Deliver customized career recommendations based 
on individual skills, interests, and goals. 
• Hybrid AI Approach: Combine collaborative filtering (peer-based recommendations) 
with LLM-powered resume parsing for accurate skill mapping. 
• Market Trend Integration: Continuously update recommendations using real-time 
labor market analytics and industry forecasts. 
• Scalable Cloud Deployment: Ensure high availability and performance through 
AWS/Azure hosting with elastic scaling. 
• Interactive Visualizations: Provide intuitive dashboards and career path maps to guide 
users in decision-making. 
• Skill Gap Analysis: Identify missing competencies and suggest targeted learning 
resources or certifications. 
• Inclusive Accessibility: Design user-friendly interfaces suitable for students, 
professionals, and career counselors.
